Admissions

Entry Requirements

  • Previous school reports or transcripts
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Completed application form

Application Process

  1. Download and complete the application form.
  2. Submit the form with required documents to the school office or email.
  3. Attend an interview/assessment (if required).
  4. Receive admission decision and pay fees.

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: 30 November 2026
  • Entrance Assessments: 10 December 2026
  • Term Begins: 15 January 2027
